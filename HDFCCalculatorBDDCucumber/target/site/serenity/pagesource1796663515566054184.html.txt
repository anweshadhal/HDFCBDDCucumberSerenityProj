<p>When your Fixed Deposit is booked with the&nbsp;<strong>quarterly / monthly</strong>&nbsp;interest pay out option, the maturity amount is the same as the principal amount. The interest amount will be credited to your account at&nbsp;<strong>quarterly / monthly</strong>&nbsp;intervals.</p>
